# FeelUp - Features Overview

FeelUp is a comprehensive wellness and mood-tracking application designed to help users monitor their emotional well-being, set personal goals, and engage with a supportive community.

## Core Features

### 1. Mood Tracking & Sharing
- **Mood Feed:** A real-time feed of mood posts from the community.
- **Mood Posts:** Create and share mood updates with emojis and custom content.
- **Privacy Controls:** Option to post publicly, privately, or anonymously.
- **Reactions & Comments:** Engage with others' posts through supportive reactions and comments.

### 2. Journaling
- **Mood Journal:** Private space for daily reflections and deep-dives into emotional states.
- **Gratitude Journaling:** Dedicated feature for recording things you're grateful for.
- **Energy Level Tracking:** Monitor energy levels (scale 1-5) alongside mood.
- **Tagging System:** Organize entries with custom tags and mood tags.
- **Entry Conversion:** Ability to convert private journal entries into community posts.

### 3. Goal Setting & Management
- **Personal Goals:** Set and track specific wellness or personal growth goals.
- **Completion Reflections:** Record your mood and a reflection note upon completing a goal.
- **Progress Tracking:** View history and stats of completed goals.

### 4. Gamification & Motivation
- **Streaks:** Track consistent activity across goals, posts, and journaling.
- **Achievements & Badges:** Earn badges for milestones (e.g., 7-day streak, social engagement, wellness goals).
- **Point System:** Accumulate points through various activities within the app.

### 5. Community & Social
- **Explore:** Discover new content and connected users within the community.
- **Community Guidelines:** Clear standards for maintaining a supportive environment.
- **Follow System:** Follow other users to keep up with their wellness journeys.
- **Messaging:** Direct communication with peers for deeper support.
- **Events:** Participate in community-wide wellness events.

### 6. Analytics & Insights
- **Mood Analytics:** Detailed visualization and analysis of mood patterns over time.
- **Daily Activity Tracking:** Keep tabs on your overall engagement and wellness habits.

### 7. User Profile & Personalization
- **Customizable Profiles:** Personalize with bios, full names, and avatars.
- **Theme Support:** Choose from different visual themes (e.g., default, dark modes).
- **Mood Colors:** Set a signature mood color for your profile.
- **Privacy Settings:** Robust controls over who can see your data and posts.

### 8. System & Support
- **Secure Authentication:** User login and profile management.
- **Terms & Privacy:** Transparent documentation of terms of service and privacy policies.
- **Support Center:** Resources and help for using the application.

## Technical Foundation
- **Frontend:** Built with Next.js (App Router), React, and Tailwind CSS.
- **Backend:** API-driven architecture with Supabase/PostgreSQL for data persistence.
- **Database Schema:** Includes dedicated tables for profiles, streaks, achievements, mood posts, reactions, comments, goals, and journal entries.
